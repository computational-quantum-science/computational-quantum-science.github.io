In the beginning of this week, we had a fantastic workshop in Würzburg to kick off our new DFG Research Unit FOR5919 “Machine Learning for Complex Quantum States”. Together with the participants and three invited speakers we discussed various topics ranging from machine learning-enhanced quantum gas experiments to agents learning to play quantum games. We’re looking forward to exciting research projects and lively meetings over the course of the next years.
